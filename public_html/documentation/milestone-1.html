Purpose

The purpose of this project is to act as a catalog for a local candle shop and will provide information such as available candles and contact information to the owner and candle maker for questions and orders.

Audience

This site is designed for people who want to purchase quality handmade candles from a small business that are unique and full of character.

Goal

The goal of this site is to provide a place where potential buyers can go and browse the catalog of a small business and be provided with information on how and where to contact the owner for questions and inquiries.

Persona

Emily Parker

Profession

Emily is an owner and operator of a small boutique that makes custom clothes and accessories for children. She has pop up shops at her local flea markets and an online shop where people can order her products. Emily understand the struggle of running a small business and does her best to support other small businesses such as her own.

Technology

Mac user, Emily, owns a 13-inch M-2 MacBook Air and an iPhone 14 Pro Max and uses these devices for her business and personal use.

Attitudes and Behaviors

Passionate about her small business Emily understands the struggles and hardships that come with owning and opperating it, because of this she does her best to support others in her same position. Although Emily enjoys the satisfaction of helping an entrepreneur like herself, she does not want to have to sacrifice quality when buying products. When exploring a site of a small business she has specific aspects she looks for like how easy it is to view the product, how easy it is to contact the owner and how easy it is to view the companies socials.

Frustrations and Needs

Emily wants to buy a candle and support a small business while simultaneously having an easy time doing it. She understands that small business websites do not have the reach and order ability that billion-dollar corporations such as Amazon and Walmart will have, but she expects them to show efforts and care. She needs a catalog site to be easy to browse and enjoyable to look at. She hates having to search to find different things such as sizes and styles of candles, how to contact the owner, where to place an order and where to find the links to follow there different social media accounts.

Goals

Emily's goal is to find a site that sells fun and interesting candles and is owned by a small business owner. She wants the site to be easy to browse and easy to find where to order.

User Story

As a candle enthusiast, I need a site where I can browse high-quality candles and do what is necessary to order them as easily as possible.
